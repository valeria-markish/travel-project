WHY TOKYO?

Tokyo, between tradition and ultramodern

Tokyo is a city that inspires the senses. Whether from its more futuristic side, full of neon, technological attractions and streets that look like an urban fashion parade, or from its more traditional side, with its gardens, palaces and vestiges of the Edo period, for example. Everything about it is superlative.
